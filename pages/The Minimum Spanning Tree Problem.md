- #[[Minimum Spanning Tree]]
- Input:
	- Connected undirected graph $G=<V,E>$
	- Cost $C_e \ge 0$ for each $e \in E$
- Output:
	- [[Spanning Tree]] $T \subset E$ minimizing total cost $C(T)  := \sum_{e \in T} C_e$
- {{embed [[Tree]]}}
- Algorithms to solve this problem #numlist
	- [[Prim's Algorithm]]
	- [[Kruskal's Algorithm]]
